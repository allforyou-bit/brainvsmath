About Brain vs Math

Brain vs Math is a free daily math playground. The idea is simple: your brain is up against the numbers, and two focused minutes a day is enough to keep winning.

What's here

  • Daily Target — the flagship puzzle. Six numbers, one target, five tiers from Warm-up to Expert. Everyone in the world gets the same board, and a new one drops at midnight.
  • Math Sprint — a 60-second adaptive speed round. How many can you solve before the bar runs out?
  • Times Tables — an interactive 12×12 chart with study lists and a quick-fire quiz.
  • Worksheet Generator — free printable practice sheets with answer keys, for teachers, tutors and parents.
  • Percentage Calculator — three everyday percent tools that show their working.
  • The blog — weekly, practical mental math technique articles.

Our principles

  • No accounts. Your streaks and stats live in your browser, on your device.
  • Free to play, free to print. Worksheets carry no watermarks or limits.
  • Fast everywhere. The whole site is hand-built, dependency-free HTML/CSS/JS — it loads in a blink on a school Chromebook or a five-year-old phone.
  • Same puzzle for everyone. Daily boards are generated from the date itself, so your result is comparable with any friend's, anywhere.
