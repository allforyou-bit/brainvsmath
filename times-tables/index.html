Tips for mastering the tables

  • Learn the easy patterns first: 2s are doubles, 10s add a zero, 5s end in 0 or 5, 11s repeat the digit (11 × 4 = 44).
  • 9s trick: the digits of every 9-fact add to 9, and the tens digit is one less than the multiplier: 9 × 7 = 63.
  • Use the swap: 8 × 3 is the same as 3 × 8 — knowing one direction halves the work.
  • Square anchors: memorize 6×6, 7×7, 8×8 and step off them: 7 × 8 = 7×7 + 7 = 56.
  • Little and often: a 10-question quiz daily beats an hour once a week. Come back tomorrow!
