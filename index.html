What is Daily Target?

Daily Target is a free daily numbers game inspired by classic arithmetic puzzles. You get six numbers and one target. Combine the numbers using addition, subtraction, multiplication and division — each result becomes a new number you can use — until you hit the target exactly. There are five tiers each day, from Warm-up to Expert, and everyone in the world gets the same puzzle. Come back tomorrow for a fresh one, keep your streak alive, and share your star line with friends.

How to play

  1. Tap a number, tap an operation (+, −, ×, ÷), then tap a second number.
  2. The two numbers merge into the result — division must be exact, and results stay positive.
  3. Reach the target exactly to earn the tier's star. Use Undo freely.
  4. Stuck? A hint reveals the first step (that tier's star turns into a half moon 🌗).
  5. Solve at least one tier to extend your daily streak. Perfect days = all five, no hints.

How to play

Reach the target number by combining the six number tiles.

  1. Tap a number → tap an operation → tap another number.
  2. They merge into a new tile you can keep using.
  3. Hit the target exactly to solve the tier. ⭐

Rules: division must divide evenly, and no step may go below 1. Five tiers per day — Warm-up to Expert. Everyone gets the same puzzle. A new one drops at midnight (your local time).
