Frequently asked questions

Are these worksheets really free?

Yes — free for classroom, tutoring and home use, with no signup, watermark or limit. Generate as many as you like.

Do worksheets include answer keys?

Yes. Keep "Include answer key" checked and the answers print on a separate final page, so you can hand out problems and keep the key.

Can I share or re-print the exact same worksheet?

Every worksheet has a code. "Copy link" gives you a URL that recreates the identical worksheet — handy for make-up work or parallel classes.

Which grades is each difficulty for?

Easy ≈ grades 1-2 (single-digit facts). Medium ≈ grades 3-4 (times tables, two-digit arithmetic). Hard ≈ grades 4-6 (multi-digit work and division facts).
